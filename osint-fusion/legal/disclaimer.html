Report Disclaimer

Orvex OSINT Fusion reports are analytical aids. They must be reviewed by a qualified human analyst before use.

No Automated Decision

Risk scores, summaries, and indicators must not be used as the sole basis for legal, HR, credit, insurance, compliance, enforcement, or operational decisions.

Source Limitations

Reports rely on public sources, third-party providers, DNS, HTTP headers, blockchain APIs, registry data, and AI summaries. Sources may be incomplete, delayed, unavailable, inaccurate, or rate-limited.

Verification Required

Users must verify sources, preserve evidence where needed, and apply professional judgment before taking action.
