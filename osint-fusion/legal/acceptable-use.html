Acceptable Use Policy

Use Orvex OSINT Fusion only for lawful defensive intelligence, cybersecurity, compliance support, fraud-risk review, and supplier due diligence.

Prohibited Uses

Do not use the platform for stalking, doxxing, harassment, intimidation, unauthorized access, exploitation, credential abuse, malware, phishing, illegal profiling, unlawful surveillance, or misuse of personal data.

Authorization

Users must have a lawful basis and, where required, authorization to analyze domains, companies, email indicators, wallets, suppliers, or identity signals.

Personal Data

Personal-data related analysis must be minimized, lawful, proportionate, and reviewed by a human before any action is taken.

Enforcement

Orvex may suspend or terminate access for abuse, suspicious use, policy violations, payment fraud, or legal risk.
